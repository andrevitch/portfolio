About

I design and build client-oriented websites for small businesses all by myself. So you don't need to think about all these technical details and leave it all to me.

For me a website is not an art, and it shouldn't be. Sure, it must be clean, aesthetic and visually appealing for clients, but what I think is more important — your website must play it's main role — sell your products or services.

Your website is your storefront and a marketing machine, no matter what business you have — repairing cars or drawing pictures.

A good website answers 3 simple questions about your business in about 5 seconds. Questions are:

  • who are you?
  • what are you offering?
  • how can client buy your product/service?

A purpose of a business website is to convert visitors into customers. And this is what I can help you with — I make nothing fancy but a working digital tool for growing your business, save your time and help your clients find your services.

Work Process

1. Discovery

You tell me about your business — your goals, services, target audience, competitors. We form the goal of your website, what problems does it solve, main Call-To-Action, your strong sides and why visitors should choose you.

2. Content

We gather all information for your website. Have you already got a website/hosting/domain, any existing content for the website (images/videos/texts/etc.)? Do you need a blog/gallery/any extra features?

3. Design

We discuss visual side of your website — what's your desired look/feel of the website? Then I give you a mock-up of your website. When you're happy with it — we go further.

4. Developing

I transform our mock-up into a real website, put it on a hosting, optimize it, test it and when it's all done — I present a website to you.

5. Support

After launching I answer all your questions about how to use/update your website. During the guarantee period you are free to ask me all the questions you have or ask for any small fixes.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is not included into making a website?

  • Any text writing, text translation
  • Any photo/video/audio content
  • Website hosting ($5-50 USD per month)
  • Domain name (about $15 per year)
  • Logo design

Does my website require maintenance?

Yes, but it's not critical to not doing any maintenance for months. What I recommend to do is a simple monthly backup and security update. Anything else is completely optional.

How do I pay?

For making a brand new website or redesigning an existing one — I ask clients to pay 30% after design phase is complete and another 70% when the whole work is done.

For website optimization I take payment only when the work is 100% done and the client is happy.

For website maintenance I charge monthly payment (price depends on what kind of services you need).

What payment methods can I use?

Currently I accept payments via PayPal only.

Can I update/change the website by myself?

Of course — yes. And I'll make you a short video about how to do this. Trust me — it's easier than you think.

Can I get any support after guarantee period is over?

Sure, we are not strangers. I can always give you small consultations or give advice about your website.
